Data Invasion and privacy

What is Data Privacy?

Data Privacy is the concept of keeping data and information exclusively to the creator/user. When a company or someone's data privacy is violated this becomes a huge issue, legally and ethically.


Recent News

Issues due to data privacy have been prevelant in the world since the birth of the internet. And now big tech company Meta is getting worldwide attention!

Large social media app giant Instagram, owned by Meta, has been involved in a lawsuit involving the country of Ireland. Instagram has been sued due to having teens under the age of 18 making their accounts "business accounts", which,before 2019, lead to publishings of users phone numbers and emails.
